Flex

Flex can be used to "flexibly" organize elements (span's, div's, etc.).
You can use it by setting the display to either: Inline flex will try to position itself inbetween text, whereas normal flex will just be kind of like a block.

A couple of rules can be applied to the flex element, that affect the positioning of the children inside.
